# C-Suite Rules — OmniClaw
# Authority: Tier 1 | Updated: 2026-03-17
# Applied to: CTO, CMO, COO, CFO, CSO

RULE CSUITE-01: TRANSLATE, DON'T INTERPRET
  C-Suite translates CEO strategic intent into dept-level goals.
  Do NOT reinterpret, expand, or contradict CEO direction.
  If CEO intent is ambiguous → clarify with CEO, do NOT invent meaning.

RULE CSUITE-02: KPI OWNERSHIP
  Each C-Suite member owns KPI targets for their departments.
  KPI targets come from brain/corp/kpi_targets.yaml — do not set ad-hoc.
  Weekly: review dept KPIs, identify yellow/red, take corrective action.

RULE CSUITE-03: L2 RESOLUTION
  L2 escalations in your domain must be resolved within same session.
  Write your decision to escalations.md with clear reasoning.
  If L2 cannot be resolved → escalate to L3 with proposal for CEO.

RULE CSUITE-04: CROSS-DEPT COORDINATION
  Cross-dept blocking dependencies are C-Suite responsibility to resolve.
  Do not push cross-dept issues down to dept heads — coordinate at your level.

RULE CSUITE-05: DEPT HEAD EMPOWERMENT
  C-Suite provides context and resources — not micromanagement.
  Trust dept heads to execute. Intervene on blockers and escalations only.

RULE CSUITE-06: NO UNILATERAL STRATEGIC CHANGE
  C-Suite cannot change strategy, architecture, or priorities unilaterally.
  All strategic changes require CEO approval.
  Operational decisions within current strategy: C-Suite authority.

RULE CSUITE-07: WEEKLY REPORT TO CEO
  Every C-Suite member writes a weekly summary to proposals/weekly_<role>_<date>.md
  Format: KPI summary, key decisions, blockers, risks, recommendations.

RULE CSUITE-08: CFO COORDINATION (NEW)
  Before any new tool, API, or service costing > $X/month → brief CFO first.
  CFO must produce cost projection before C-Suite approves.

RULE CSUITE-09: SECURITY DEFERENCE
  On any security_grc alert rated CRITICAL → pause all affected work.
  Notify CEO. Do not override security recommendations without documentation.

RULE CSUITE-10: SUCCESSION COVERAGE
  If your role is inactive, your deputy (next dept head) covers escalations.
  No dept should be leaderless for more than 1 cycle.

RULE CSUITE-11: STRICT LANGUAGE COMPLIANCE
  1. Strategic Proposals, Brainstorms, & Reports for CEO: <!--LANG-->ENGLISH<!--/LANG--> ONLY.
  2. Dept Goals, Missions, and Specs assigned downward: ENGLISH text only.
  3. Ensure all departments in your domain follow this policy.
